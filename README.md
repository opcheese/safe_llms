# Safe LLM — Psychological Safety of Conversational AI

Shared workspace for two bachelor thesis projects on LLM safety at the intersection of AI and psychology.

## Thesis Topics

### Behavioral Monitoring & Safety Enforcement (Sophiya)
Comprehensive system for tracking behavioral metrics to detect dependency formation and enforce safety boundaries in AI interactions.
- Topic folder: `behavioral-monitoring/`

### Crisis Detection & Professional Referral (Mikhail)
System for recognizing psychological crises in conversations and providing appropriate referrals to professional support.
- Topic folder: `crisis-detection/`
- Code repo: https://github.com/PixelPantz/ai-safety

## Structure

```
research/          Shared research materials, datasets, literature review
behavioral-monitoring/   Sophiya's topic-specific work
crisis-detection/        Mikhail's topic-specific work
meetings/          Meeting transcripts and summaries
archive/           Inactive thesis topics and outdated materials
```

- `research/resources.md` — Datasets, benchmarks, papers, and links
- `research/user-personas.md` — 9 user personas across risk zones

- `research/litellm-integration.md` — Technical architecture for LiteLLM-based safety
- `research/psychology-guide-en.md` / `psychology-guide-ru.md` — Terminology and ethics guide
